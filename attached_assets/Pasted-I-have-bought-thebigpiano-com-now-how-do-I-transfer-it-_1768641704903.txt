### **STEP 2: Deploy to Cloudflare Pages**

1. Go to **[dash.cloudflare.com](https://dash.cloudflare.com)**
2. Sign in (or create account - it's free)
3. Click **"Workers & Pages"** in left sidebar
4. Click **"Create application"**
5. Select **"Pages"** tab
6. Click **"Connect to Git"**
7. Click **"Connect GitHub"**
8. Authorize Cloudflare to access your GitHub
9. Select your **`the-big-piano`** repository
10. Click **"Begin setup"**

**Build settings:**
- **Project name:** `the-big-piano`
- **Production branch:** `main`
- **Framework preset:** None (or "Static HTML")
- **Build command:** (leave empty)
- **Build output directory:** `/` or `public` (depending on where your HTML files are)

11. Click **"Save and Deploy"**

**Cloudflare will build and deploy your site. Wait ~2 minutes.**

You'll get a URL like: `https://the-big-piano.pages.dev`

---

### **STEP 3: Connect Your Domain (thebigpiano.com)**

#### **Part A: Point Domain to Cloudflare**

1. Go to your **domain registrar** (where you bought thebigpiano.com - GoDaddy, Namecheap, etc.)
2. Find **"DNS Settings"** or **"Nameservers"**
3. Change nameservers to Cloudflare's:
```
   amin.ns.cloudflare.com
   kurt.ns.cloudflare.com
```
   *(Cloudflare will give you the exact ones when you add the domain)*

#### **Part B: Add Domain to Cloudflare**

1. In Cloudflare dashboard, click **"Add site"**
2. Enter: `thebigpiano.com`
3. Select **Free plan**
4. Cloudflare will scan your DNS records
5. Click **"Continue"**
6. Cloudflare shows you nameservers - copy these to your registrar (from Part A)
7. Click **"Done, check nameservers"**

**Wait 5-60 minutes for DNS propagation.**

#### **Part C: Connect Domain to Your Pages Project**

1. Go back to **Workers & Pages**
2. Click on your **`the-big-piano`** project
3. Click **"Custom domains"** tab
4. Click **"Set up a custom domain"**
5. Enter: `thebigpiano.com`
6. Click **"Continue"**
7. Cloudflare will automatically configure DNS
8. Also add: `www.thebigpiano.com` (repeat steps, it will redirect to main)

**Wait ~5 minutes for SSL certificate to provision.**
