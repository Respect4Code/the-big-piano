Thiel’s core thesis is that **Value Creation (X)** and **Value Capture (Y)** are independent. The airline industry creates massive value (X) but captures almost none of it (Y=0.2%) because of perfect competition. Google creates less total value than the global air travel industry, but captures a massive percentage (Y=25%+) because it is a monopoly.

**The Lie:** "I am building a parenting app or a storybook app."

This is a market of one.

If you position this as a **Private Family Archive**, you capture value through high switching costs.
